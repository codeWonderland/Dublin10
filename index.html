A decade of Champlain Abroad Dublin, a weekend of craic in Burlington, Vermont. Join us on June 8th to kick off a weekend of reminiscing and celebrating 10 years of Champlain College's presence in Dublin, Ireland.

Please take a moment to share a favorite photo or brief memory about your Dublin experience!

If you have a reflection you would like to share on this website and with all attendees as part of the weekend program, please submit it here or email dublin10@champlain.edu with the details. Your memory could be in the form of a story, poem, song, video or work of art.
